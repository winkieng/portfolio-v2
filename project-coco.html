CoCo Fresh Tea & Juice_

CoCo Fresh Tea & Juice is an international bubble tea brand known for its vibrant flavours and playful personality.

The client requested five themed posters and matching social media visuals for seasonal drink campaign.

Each design captured the flavour, mood, and personality of the drink, focusing less on strict brand guidelines and more on emotional connection through colour, composition, and storytelling.

explanation ×

One of the customer concern is that they think the current posters are too simple. They want something more eye catching comparing with other competitors.

Customer's Goal_

1. Promote seasonal drinks through visually distinctive posters and social media designs that reflect each beverage's unique flavour and mood.

2. Enhance brand engagement by using colour, typography, and layout to connect emotionally with younger audiences.

3. Ensure cross-platform consistency so that each design works effectively both in-store and across digital channels.

Design Challenge

• The biggest challenge was finding the right balance between creative expression and brand recognition.

• CoCo already has a strong visual identity, but the campaign needed to give each drink its own unique personality.

• The exploration focused on how colour, typography, and imagery could communicate the taste and emotion of each drink while keeping them connected under a cohesive design system.

Approach_

step_01

Research & Taste Testing

Explored each drink's flavour, colour, and emotion by tasting them in person and observing their ingredients and textures.

step_02

Moodboard Development

Created moodboards to establish individual visual directions for each drink based on flavour and atmosphere.

step_03

Poster & Social Media Design

Designed poster for each drink, then adapted the layouts for Instagram posts and stories to maintain consistency.

step_04

Mockup Visualization

Applied the final designs to in-store and digital mockups to demonstrate their real-world impact and visual harmony.

Design Process_

Mango Slushies_

Concept

A vibrant, summer-themed poster featuring bright blue sea and sky, designed to create a refreshing and tropical atmosphere for the mango slushie.

Design Approach

• A bold yellow font with black shadow and white outline was used for the title to create a fun and eye-catching effect.

• The yellow symbolizes the color of mango, while the white represents the sago topping or coconut milk in the drink.

• Smaller text also follows the sunny yellow palette to maintain consistency.

• A warm-toned background reflects summer sunlight, and subtle stars in the corners add a playful, seasonal touch.

Strawberry Series_

Concept

A picnic-themed poster for the three strawberry drinks, capturing the fresh and vibrant feeling of summer and spring — the perfect seasons for outdoor picnics.

Design Approach

• The composition includes a blue sky, green grass, a red-and-white picnic mat, and a picnic basket to reinforce the outdoor picnic setting.

• A red serif typeface was chosen for the title to represent strawberries, while white text was used for the subtitle and body copy to enhance readability and reference the milk, yogurt, or macchiato elements found in the drinks.

Grape Dream_

Concept

A dreamy, cosmic theme inspired by the blend of macchiato and grape slush, which creates a mixture of purple and white reminiscent of the universe.

Design Approach

• A purple galaxy backdrop was created, surrounded by grape elements to enhance flavor association.

• The title uses white with a purple highlight to mirror the blended layers of the drink, while other text elements remain in white for clarity.

• A supplementary social media sub-page was developed to present the ingredients and clarify the composition of the Grape Dream drink.

Brown Sugar Series_

Concept

A warm autumn-themed poster featuring cozy brown tones and seasonal elements to highlight the rich, caramelized flavour of the brown sugar drinks.

Design Approach

• Warm brown and cream tones were used throughout to evoke the comforting feeling of autumn.

• Typography choices reflect the premium, artisanal quality of the brown sugar series.

• The design emphasizes the drink's signature tiger stripes pattern created by the brown sugar syrup.

Matcha Series_

Concept

A serene, zen-inspired poster that captures the essence of Japanese tea culture and the calming nature of matcha.

Design Approach

• Soft green tones and minimalist design elements reflect the peaceful nature of matcha.

• Clean typography and generous white space create a sense of calm and sophistication.

• The design balances traditional Japanese aesthetics with modern appeal.

Outcome & Deliverables_

The final deliverables included five posters and a set of social media assets that formed a cohesive yet flexible visual system.

Each design expressed the drink's unique flavor through a corresponding visual mood, improving recognition and engagement across Instagram and in-store displays.

Mockups were created to demonstrate scalability, clarity, and consistent impact across print and digital formats.

Reflection_

This project was created during the early rise of AI tools in design. AI supported the initial ideation phase by accelerating mood exploration and enabling rapid theme variations. However, achieving visual consistency across all five posters still required deliberate design decisions beyond AI's capabilities.

The project reinforced how AI can enhance creativity while highlighting the importance of a structured workflow, clear artistic direction, and human refinement in producing a cohesive final outcome.
