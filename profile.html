Design is what I do, Drawing is who I am

A visual designer based in Vancouver, BC with a passion for creating meaningful and engaging digital experiences. With a background in Interactive Arts & Technology from Simon Fraser University, I blend creativity with strategic thinking to craft designs that resonate.

I believe in the power of design to tell stories, solve problems, and connect people with the world around them. Whether it's building brand identities, designing packaging, or creating digital experiences, I approach every project with curiosity and attention to detail.

When I'm not pushing pixels, you'll find me sketching in coffee shops, exploring local bookstores, or seeking inspiration through travel. I'm always excited to collaborate on projects that challenge conventions and create lasting impact.
