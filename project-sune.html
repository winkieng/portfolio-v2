SUNÉ_

SUNÉ is a conceptual energy drink brand inspired by the Japanese fox spirit, Kitsune. The project explores how cultural symbolism can be translated into a bold, contemporary packaging system for young, active consumers.

I designed the bottle packaging, cap stickers, and sachets for three flavours: Mango Pineapple Punch, Cherry Ramune, and Grape Ramune.

Reflection_

This project marked my first experience designing packaging that required direct collaboration with a print company, introducing practical challenges beyond digital design. While the brand identity relied heavily on energetic, cyberpunk-inspired RGB colours, the final production required conversion into CMYK for print.

Balancing the client's visual expectations with technical print limitations became a key part of the process. Through multiple colour iterations and test adjustments, I explored ways to preserve the intensity and vibrancy of the original designs while ensuring they remained effective and consistent when printed.

This experience deepened my understanding of colour systems and print production constraints, while highlighting the importance of adaptability in real-world design workflows. It strengthened my ability to bridge creative vision with technical execution, resulting in packaging that remains visually impactful both on screen and in physical form.
