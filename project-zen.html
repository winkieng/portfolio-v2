Zen Maker Lab_

This project involved creating brand illustrations and promotional materials for Zen Maker Lab, a Vancouver-based STEAM education company serving students from kindergarten through grade 12. The work focused on developing a cohesive mascot character and visual assets that would appeal to young learners while maintaining brand consistency.

The goal was to produce engaging illustrations for achievement stickers, activity booklets, and promotional materials that celebrate student accomplishments and encourage participation in summer camps and educational programs.

Gary the Octopus

Following the research phase, the octopus character was named Gary. Gary features a distinctive design with a large, round head, expressive eyes, and long tentacles. To enhance its unique appearance, dots were incorporated on the head, aligning with the brand colour palette.

In response to the educational needs of junior classes, Dash Robot was introduced to facilitate children's lessons. To make the robot more approachable and engaging for young learners, a vibrant and whimsical cartoon rendition was crafted.
